The Story

Patterson Consulting is a group of engineers, architects, and scientists led by Josh Patterson. We work with mid-market and enterprise companies to build data platforms, data pipelines, and generative AI applications.

Patterson Consulting is built around the philosophy that to understand how to build a data platform to drive business growth you have to understand the core business. We apply traditional business analysis coupled with deep expertise in platform engineering, data engineering, and artificial intelligence.

Engineers on our team have experience in big data, cloud platforms, MLOps platforms, data integration, traditional machine learning, and generative AI.

Multiple members of our team have published books in the software industry as well.

Our team is comprised of engineers who have decades of experience in data platforms and who will dig deep into your hardest problems to find the right solution for your company.

The Value of Platform Engineering

Great platform architecture and engineering is a competitive advantage to all business today because if we cannot scale our business better than "linearly adding more people as we add business", then our data platform is not doing its job.

Great data platforms help forward looking companies grow their business faster than their competitors, and at Patterson Consulting we deliver those data platforms.

Why Use Our Platform Engineering Team?

Nearly all business today is dependant on data. To do better than a manual process for most businesses, you need a platform that your organization trusts to deliver correct data and deliver the data in a timeframe that is relevant to the business process.

Patterson Consulting helps you accelerate your business by using a classical business analysis combined with expert platform engineering architecture. This means that Patterson Consulting gets to know the core key indicators that drive your business and then architects a data platform that focuses on these key indicators as a measure of success.

This means that at Patterson Consulting we don't do platform architecture for the sake of more technology -- we design data platforms that improve your line of business.

If you are an organization that resorts to manual processes across the company because your teams don't trust the data from your data platform, then you can use our services.
