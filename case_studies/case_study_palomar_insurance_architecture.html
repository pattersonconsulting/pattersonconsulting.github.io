Enabling Property Insurance Premium Growth Through Platform Architecture

How Palomar Overcame Data Fragmentation to Drive Operational Efficiency and Scalability

Palomar's Platform Challenge

Palomar’s fragmented data environment and reliance on manual processes are creating operational inefficiencies, scalability constraints, and delays across key functions such as underwriting, actuarial, claims, and finance.

To meet its growth objectives, the company must implement a unified, scalable data platform that automates workflows, accelerates decision-making, and improves overall cash flow management.

Patterson Consulting's Solution

Patterson Consulting engaged with Palomar in a Knowledge Work Architecture Review engagement that:

  • Helped make plans to overhaul its fragmented data environment, which was impeding growth and operational scalability
  • Patterson Consulting addressed critical issues such as broken internal integrations, manual billing reconciliation, and delays in external data processing
  • The resulting modern data platform—built on Azure, Snowflake, and DBT—streamlines information flows, accelerates product launches, reduces claims processing time, and enhances actuarial efficiency

Palomar Insurance is a forward-thinking specialty insurer offering a diversified portfolio across five key product lines: Earthquake, Inland Marine and Other Property, Casualty, Fronting, and Crop. Palomar faces significant challenges stemming from a fragmented data environment, which hampers operational efficiency, inflates costs, and constrains scalability—critical barriers to achieving its PLMR2X growth objectives.

In today’s knowledge-driven economy, where technology and data are key drivers of competitive advantage, this disjointed infrastructure limits the company’s ability to make timely, data-informed decisions and adapt to market demands. As investors and stakeholders increasingly evaluate companies based on growth potential and technological agility, Palomar must invest in integrated data systems to streamline processes, reduce manual workload, and support scalable, efficient operations aligned with long-term strategic goals.

Palomar's Data Platform Challenge

Palomar's efforts to modernize its data platform and knowledge work architecture are hindered by a fragmented data environment that creates significant inefficiencies and scalability challenges. The company’s reliance on manual processes, such as the broken internal data integration for financial reconciliation and inefficient TPA and MGA data management, slows decision-making and increases operational costs. Additionally, the lack of a unified data source forces heavy reliance on developers for data extraction, which limits both scalability and innovation across core business functions like underwriting, actuarial, claims, and finance.

To achieve its ambitious growth targets, Palomar must address these challenges by implementing a robust, integrated data platform. Key imperatives include building scalable infrastructure to speed product launches, automating billing processes to eliminate reconciliation inefficiencies, enhancing actuarial efficiency for better cash flow modeling, and reducing claims processing latency. By overcoming these barriers, Palomar can streamline operations, improve cash flow management, and better position itself for continued growth and expansion.

Project Results

Patterson Consulting partnered with Palomar Insurance to re-architect its fragmented data environment, addressing critical inefficiencies that were hindering growth and scalability. By identifying key gaps—including broken internal system integrations, manual billing reconciliation, and latency in external data management—Patterson delivered a modernized data architecture (Azure, Snowflake, and DBT) aligned with Palomar’s growth strategy. The new platform streamlines information flows, enables faster product launches, reduces claims latency, and enhances actuarial efficiency.

Enabling Premium Growth Through Strategic Technology Investment

Palomar is leveraging technology as a strategic enabler, transforming data into actionable insights that accelerate the conversion of sales into cash. This operational efficiency not only improves cash flow but also frees up capital for reinvestment in growth initiatives such as marketing and customer acquisition. As a result, Palomar strengthens its market position, enhances its valuation, and creates a solid foundation for sustainable expansion—making growth a central pillar of its strategic vision.

Why Patterson Consulting?

Palomar selected Patterson Consulting to redesign its data platform architecture due to the firm’s unique, outcome-driven philosophy that prioritizes productivity over technology for its own sake. Patterson approaches data architecture with a clear focus on enabling knowledge work by improving workflow efficiency and team coordination—ultimately transforming raw data into revenue-generating insights. Their methodology emphasizes how each architectural decision impacts business output and platform productivity, aligning closely with Palomar’s growth and operational goals.

With proven frameworks that support seamless data integration, daily-refresh information access, and end-to-end workflow continuity, Patterson Consulting delivered scalable, modern data platforms grounded in practical business outcomes, making them an ideal partner for Palomar’s transformation.
