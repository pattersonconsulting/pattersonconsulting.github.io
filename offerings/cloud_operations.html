Managed Support and Operations

The Patterson Consulting team has the background and experience to help you move to the cloud safely, securely, and efficiently. From platform migrations to security reviews, our team can work with you to build a plan to migrate old applications to the cloud or create net new applications.

Patterson Consulting also provides foundational support with our managed services offerings in:

  • Kubernetes
  • Kubeflow
  • Kafka
  • Hadoop
Either on-premise, or on the cloud of your choice. Our managed services team can optimize and protect your investment in your platform. Protecting this investment is critical to realizing value from data platform operations and machine learning projects.

Patterson Consulting offers integration services (in the cloud and on-premise) for systems such as identity-mangement in Kubernetes with Active Directory and Kerberos. Customized identity integration commonly is critical for enterprise platform systems to be useful to the Fortune 500.

Further, we can manage entire machine learning pipelines for your organization and keep models up-to-date, secure, and serving predictions to online applications. Your team will benefit from our team's industry leading publications and experience with these foundational technologies.
