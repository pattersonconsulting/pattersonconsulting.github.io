Great Architecture by a Great Team

Modernizing a data platform is not an easy process and you should trust the architecture of your next-generation data platform to a team that has the track record of success on some of the most complex platforms in the Global 2000.

The team has experience building distributed systems for major power grid organizations, major chip manufacturers, 2 world telecoms, and 5 of the largest banks in the world.

The Patterson Consulting team has also authored multiple O'Reilly books on data and platforms, along with peer-reviewed academic papers in distributed systems. This is the team you can trust to migrate your legacy data platform to the a modern Databricks data lakehouse.
