Managed Operations with Snowflake

Snowflake has many of the traditional DBA tasks already built in. However, the still requires expertise for operations. Our Managed Service for Snowflake is based around providing accelerated and efficient services for:

  • Administration
  • Provisioning
  • Automation
  • Development support

This allows you to focus on your core business problems without worrying about the complexities of operating the data platform. This efficiency equates to time and costs savings in your line of business as well.
